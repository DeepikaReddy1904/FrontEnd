After effects

Adobe After Effects is a digital visual effects, motion graphics, and compositing application used in the post-production process of film, video games, and television production.

Adobe animate

Adobe Animate is a multimedia authoring and computer animation program used for creating vector graphics, animations, games, and web applications, supporting both HTML5 and legacy Flash content.

Photoshop

Adobe Photoshop is a powerful image editing software widely used for photo retouching, graphic design, and digital art, offering advanced tools for manipulating and enhancing images.
